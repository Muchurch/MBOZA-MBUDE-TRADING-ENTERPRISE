ABOUT US


Established in April 2025, our company stands as a proudly South African, wholly black-owned enterprise with a Level 1 BBBEE status. Located at 33 Powell Road, Morningside, KwaZulu-Natal, we are committed to driving inclusive growth and transformation within our industry. With a strong foundation built on integrity, innovation, and excellence, we offer a wide range of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of clients across South Africa. Our operations extend nationwide, allowing us to deliver consistent quality and value wherever our clients are located. As a forward-thinking business, we are dedicated to empowering local communities, fostering meaningful partnerships, and contributing to the country’s economic development. Our team is passionate about what we do and brings together deep industry knowledge, strategic insight, and a customer-first mindset to every project we undertake.

VISION

To be a leader in property improvements and renovations by delivering precision construction and elegant renovations, shaping South Africa's urban and rural landscapes for future generations.

MISSION

- To provide innovative and high-quality construction and renovation services that exceed client expectations.
- To maintain a strong focus on precision and elegant design in every project.
- To contribute positively to community development through sustainable building practices.

CORE VALUES

1. Precision: We prioritize exactness in every aspect of our work to ensure high standards and client satisfaction.
2. Elegance: We believe in creating aesthetically pleasing environments that enhance functionality and style.
3. Integrity: Conducting business with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
4. Innovation: Continuously seeking new methods and technologies to improve our services.
5. Sustainability: Committed to environmentally friendly practices in our construction and renovation projects.

LEADERSHIP TEAM

Mbulelo Mbili – Director

brings extensive business acumen and leadership experience to the company. As the founding director, he plays a pivotal role in shaping the strategic direction and long-term vision of the enterprise. His entrepreneurial mindset and deep understanding of business development have been instrumental in positioning Mboza Mbude Trading Enterprise as a trusted name in the construction and renovation industry. Under his guidance, the company has built strong relationships with clients, partners, and stakeholders across South Africa.

Simphiwe Radebe – Operations Manager

leads the operational execution of all projects with precision and professionalism. With a strong background in construction, renovations, infrastructure development, and quantity surveying, he ensures that every project is delivered to the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficiency. His expertise in managing site teams, resources, and timelines allows Mboza Mbude Trading Enterprise to consistently meet and exceed client expectations. Simphiwe’s attention to detail and problem-solving skills make him a critical asset in the successful execution of complex construction projects. Together, our leadership team exemplifies the values of integrity, accountability, and excellence driving the company forward as a leader in the built environment.
